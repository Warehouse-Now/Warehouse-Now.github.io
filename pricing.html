Our Pricing Breakdown

We believe in transparency. Below is our approach to pricing:

Core Software
(Fixed monthly cost)

Our core software powers both our web based WMS and runs on our handheld scanners.

The Scanners
(Fixed monthly cost per scanner)

We set up and configure our handheld pistol-grip scanners which we lease to you.

Onboarding
(One off cost based on requirements)

Importing existing data you may have, barcoding your warehouse, integrating with your accountancy software and staff training.

Support
(Fixed monthly cost)

Ongoing support for your staff to ensure that we're on hand if you ever need us :)
